YOUR STYLE. OUR CRAFT.

Since 2007, Republic of Florence has created premium leather carry goods that reflect both heritage and intention. Designed in Australia and handcrafted in Italy by skilled artisans, our pieces blend timeless design with quiet confidence. We use vegetable-tanned leatherfor its natural beauty, durability, and character — a slower, more considered choice that honours the craft and tradition we come from.
